OUR GOAL

Throughout our journey we would like to stick to our passion of providing high quality and comfortable products for your pups, in order to enjoy outdoor adventures.

People love adventure, and so do dogs. However, you may be hesitant to bring your dog on a muddy, wet, dirty hike. Who wants to clean off a hairy animal after it’s been running around outdoors? Nevermind the brown pawprints all over your brand new couch. Sadly, this can cause you to miss out on fun opportunities to exercise with your pet, and memories that would last you a lifetime.

If this is your dog, then these are the products for you!


If you are constantly faced with these problems, then our products will make you and your dog’s lives easier. Our Goal at Ruffwear is to provide comfortable and high-quality products for dogs to use in order to freely experience outdoor adventures with their owners. Our company will provide the necessities for you and your best bud to start your own adventures!
