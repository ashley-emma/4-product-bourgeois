RUFFWEAR DOG GEAR

OUR GOAL

Throughout our journey we would like to stick to our passion of providing high quality and comfortable products for your pups, in order to enjoy outdoor adventures.

If your dog is as adventurous as this pup here, then our line of Ruffwear Dog Gear has just the products for you!


People love adventure, and so do dogs. However, you may be hesitant to bring your dog on a muddy, wet, dirty hike. Who wants to clean off a hairy animal after it’s been running around outdoors? Nevermind the brown pawprints all over your brand new couch. Sadly, this can cause you to miss out on fun opportunities to exercise with your pet, and memories that would last you a lifetime.

If you are constantly faced with these problems, then our products will make you and your dog’s lives easier. Our Goal at Ruffwear is to provide comfortable and high-quality products for dogs to use in order to freely experience outdoor adventures with their owners. Our company will provide the necessities for you and your best bud to start your own adventures!

Track Jacket Features

Our Track Jacket is made of a high-visability blaze orange fabric that is reflective in low light conditions. Built with durable, waterproof polyester fabric, the Track Jacket has a lightweight, streamlined design that's quick and easy to put on your pup. This product is perfect walks at night!

Float Coat Features

Our reflective life jacket is easily adjustable for dogs of all sizes! It is avaliable in 3 colors and all are water compatable. Based off of the 305 reviews on our website, 95% of the people who own this product rate it 5 out of 5 stars. This product is perfect for days on the lake with your pup!

Our all-terrain paw wear is easily breathable while keeping dirt and mud out. This product is a great option for protecting your dog's paws while on hikes. They also have a reflective trim to keep track of your dog. The Grip Trex Boots come in 3 colors and are available in sets of 2 or 4.
